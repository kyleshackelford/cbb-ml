College Basketball Model Picks

This table shows daily college basketball spread predictions generated by a probabilistic statistical model. The model receives statistical description of teams as input and generates a conditional distribution of expected scoring margins. This page is updated once per day and is intended for informational purposes.

How to read the table

  • home team: home team (irrevelant for neutral court games)
  • away team: visiting team (irrevelant for neutral court games)
  • market spread: market spread with regard to home team at time of prediction
  • market odds: market betting odds with regard to home team at time of prediction
  • model consensus: ensemble consensus prediction with regard to home team (neg-home team favored; pos-away team underdog)
  • model P(cover): predicted probability that the home team covers the market spread
  • model edge: model probability minus the market odds implied probability (expressed as percentage). Edge is based on uncalibrated probabilities and should be interpreted as a ranking signal
  • model fair odds: odds implied by the model probability estimate for given market spread
  • # members cover: # of model ensemble members predicting > 50% chance of home team cover (38 total)

Model status

Predicted probabilities are currently uncalibrated and should be interpreted as relative confidence rather than exact win rates. A formal out-of-sample calibration step will be added as additional data accumulates.
